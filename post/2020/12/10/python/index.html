Introduction

Do you dream in codes? Do you ever think about making math harder than it needs to be? Then this post is the right one for you!

In this post, I'll be showing a couple of ways to do simple arithmetic in python and R but with a twist.

Yay Math

As a refresher, here are some examples of how to run some basic codes:

2+2
## [1] 4
4*67899
## [1] 271596
2347891234/23
## [1] 102082228

But why would you want to settle for numbers when you can write everything out? Here is an example in R:

twothousand <- c(2000)
fivethousandsixhundred <- c(5600)
twothousand*fivethousandsixhundred
## [1] 11200000
twothousand/fivethousandsixhundred
## [1] 0.3571429
(2*twothousand) - (5*fivethousandsixhundred)/2
## [1] -10000

And because R isn't enough, here it is in python:
